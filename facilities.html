Follow us on:

Each facility is scheduled for usage. The club opens at 6am and closes at 10pm Mon-Sat and 10am till 4pm on Sundays. Studio halls have scheduled sessions that last 1 hr each. Instructors/mentors are present during each session. The lap pool is available for all opening times. The activity pool has schedules for certain activities and can accommodate all ages. The crèche charges the fees on hourly basis. There is a package deal available for members who would need a crèche by default.

Each facility is equipped with a login system to log user usage. At entry and exit, the users scan their id cards. The time and date is logged in the system. Guests are issued with temporary ids and linked with the member they are accompanying. Typical details like their name and contact details are noted. They are mailed membership offers if they happen to use the club often.
